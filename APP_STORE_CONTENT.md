### Description (4000 characters max)

PantryIQ is your complete kitchen and health management system, powered by AI. Never wonder what to make, forget what you have, or lose track of your health goals again.

**Pantry Intelligence**
Scan barcodes or take photos to instantly add items to your smart pantry. PantryIQ tracks quantities, expiry dates, and nutritional information for every item. Know exactly what you have, where it is, and when items are expiring.

**Chef Claude — Your Personal AI Kitchen Assistant**
Ask Chef Claude anything about food, recipes, or nutrition. With access to your pantry inventory, health goals, and cooking equipment, Claude suggests personalized recipes using what you have. Choose from 6 personality modes — from friendly Coach Mode to dramatic Gordon Ramsay Mode to calm Zen philosophy. Every conversation is private and stays on your device.

**Low Carb & Keto Focused**
Designed from the ground up for low carb and keto lifestyles. Track net carbs, manage macros, estimate ketosis levels, and get smart suggestions that align with your goals. Every recipe includes carb counts and keto ratings.

**Complete Health Tracking**
Log meals with full macro tracking, measure weight and body composition, track fasting periods, monitor water intake, and log supplements. See beautiful graphs showing your progress over time. Celebrate milestones with achievements and streaks.

**Recipe Management**
Discover and save recipes, import recipes from any website, scale recipes instantly, get real-time cooking timers, and rate dishes for future reference. Cook Mode guides you step-by-step through preparation.

**Family Sharing**
Share your pantry and shopping list with family members. Sync across all devices, vote on family meals, and coordinate who's shopping and cooking.

**All Your Data Stays Private**
Your health and nutritional data never leaves your device. Optional API keys (Claude, USDA, etc.) are stored locally only. PantryIQ respects your privacy completely.

**Designed for iOS**
Beautiful, intuitive interface following Apple's Human Interface Guidelines. Optimized for all iPhone sizes. Works in Dark Mode and Light Mode. Accessible with Dynamic Type support.

## Privacy Policy

### Introduction
PantryIQ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our mobile application.

### Information We Collect

**Personal Information You Provide:**
- Profile information (name, age, height)
- Health data (weight, body composition, health goals)
- Dietary preferences and restrictions
- Photos (pantry, food, health progress)
- API keys you provide for optional integrations
- Optional email for customer support

**Information We Generate:**
- Meal logs and nutritional tracking data
- Pantry inventory records
- Recipes you create or import
- Shopping history and preferences
- Fasting sessions and health measurements

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- App version and usage patterns

### How We Use Your Information

- **To Provide Services:** Enable core features like pantry tracking, meal logging, recipe suggestions, and health tracking
- **To Improve Services:** Understand feature usage and improve the app experience
- **For AI Processing:** Send only information you choose to share to Claude API for recipe suggestions and photo identification
- **For Support:** Contact you about issues or questions you raise

### Data Location & Storage

**On Your Device:**
- All pantry items, meal logs, health measurements, photos, and settings are stored exclusively on your local device using standard iOS storage
- Data persists through app updates and continues working if you delete and reinstall the app

**Optional Cloud Services:**
- Family sharing syncs data between family members' devices only
- You control what data is shared via family sharing settings

**Third-Party Services:**
- When you provide API keys, you authorize direct connection to those services
- Claude API key: For recipe suggestions and photo identification
- USDA Food Database: For nutritional information (optional)
- You can remove any API key at any time in Settings

### Data You Don't Share

PantryIQ NEVER:
- Sells your data to any third party
- Shares your health information without permission
- Tracks your location (only your permission)
- Uses your data for advertising
- Requires creating an account or signing in
- Requires any personal information beyond what you choose to enter

### Data Security

- All data is encrypted at rest on your device
- Network communication uses industry-standard HTTPS encryption
- API keys are never logged or transmitted unnecessarily
- We recommend keeping your iOS version up to date for security patches

### Deleting Your Data

You can delete all your data anytime in Settings:
- "Reset Pantry" — Delete all pantry items
- "Reset Meal Log" — Delete all meal and food entries
- "Reset Everything" — Delete all data except API keys
- API keys are never auto-deleted for your convenience

### Children's Privacy

PantryIQ is not intended for users under 13. If we learn we've collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it.

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information
- Opt out of data collection features
- Export your data in standard formats

To exercise these rights, contact support@pantryiq.app

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or app notification. Your continued use of PantryIQ after changes constitutes acceptance.

### Contact Us

Questions about your privacy? Contact us:

## App Icon Design Brief

### Concept
A deep navy pantry door slightly open with green glowing light coming from inside, suggesting the warmth, organization, and intelligence within the app.

### Design Requirements
- **Background:** Deep navy (#0A1628) matching app theme
- **Central Element:** Stylized pantry door slightly ajar (about 30-40% open)
- **Light Effect:** Green glow (#2ECC71) emanating from inside the pantry
- **Text:** PantryIQ text below or integrated into design
- **Style:** Modern, clean, professional
- **Scalability:** Must read clearly at 1024x1024px and scale down to 20x20px minimum

### Color Palette
- Primary: Navy #0A1628
- Accent: Green #2ECC71
- Door: Slightly lighter navy or charcoal
- Glow: Green with subtle glow effect

### No-No's
- Avoid generic refrigerator/pantry clip art
- Avoid overly complex details that disappear at small sizes
- Avoid too much text
- Avoid colors outside the brand palette
